About Me

Hello, my name is Mohamed Deria. I'm a student at Seattle Central college/Yearup. Prior to joining the program, I was employed in the healthcare sector as a direct support professional and aided vulnerable adults with mental disabilities.

Working in that field has taught me the importance of having compassion, helping others in need, and being a selfless human being. I've also had the opportunity to gain some valuable knowledge from that experience, like the ability to work well in teams, have great communication, and learning how to problem-solve.

I'm currently studying in the QA track in software development to gain more technical skills such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My hope is to gain enough experience and knowledge to become a qualified professional in that field. When I'm not preoccupied with work/school I love to travel, workout, and learn how to DJ/produce music.
